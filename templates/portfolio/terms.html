Piracy Policy

At Jaysoft Technology, we are committed to protecting our intellectual property and the rights of our clients. We take software piracy seriously and have established the following policies to combat illegal use and distribution of our software products.

Definition of Piracy

Software piracy includes, but is not limited to, the unauthorized copying, distribution, modification, or use of our software products. This includes distributing pirated copies of our software via any means, including physical media or digital platforms.

Prevention Measures

We employ various technical measures to protect our software from unauthorized use and distribution. This includes software licensing, encryption, and regular audits of our distribution channels.

Reporting Piracy

If you suspect or have evidence of software piracy involving our products, please report it to us immediately at piracy@jaysofttech.com. We take all reports seriously and will investigate any allegations of piracy.

Consequences of Piracy

Individuals or organizations found to be engaging in software piracy will face legal action, including but not limited to, civil and criminal penalties. We are committed to protecting our intellectual property and will pursue all available legal remedies against those who violate our rights.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our piracy policy, please contact us at support@jaysofttech.com.
